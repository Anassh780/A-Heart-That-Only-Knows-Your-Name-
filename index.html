Dear Zainab Fatima,

I don't know if I will ever be able to explain everything my heart feels for you, but today I want to try. I have kept many things inside my heart for a long time, and sometimes silence becomes too painful. So I am writing these words honestly — not perfectly, but sincerely.

Zainab, I know you have been through things that changed you. I can see how sometimes you become quiet, distant, and lost in your own thoughts. Sometimes you choose to be alone; sometimes it feels as though you hide your feelings from everyone. Maybe you are protecting your heart. Maybe life hurt you in ways I cannot fully understand.

And I respect that deeply.

But there is one question that has lived in my heart for a very long time —

Can you not become normal with just one person… the one who still loves you after all these years?

The one who stayed even during your hardest times.

The one who always tried to make you smile.

The one who never cared about who you were or what people said.

The one who still carries feelings for you no matter how much time passes.

The one who prays for you quietly.

The one who cried because of you.

The one who never wants to see you hurt or struggling.

Even if you don't talk much…

Even if you stay distant…

Even if the world leaves you alone…

I would still stay beside you.

Because loving you was never something temporary for me.

Zainab, I also have a heart. I also have feelings. For a long time I tried to hide them because I didn't want to make things difficult for you. But the truth is, I cannot pretend anymore. My heart still belongs to you, and living without you feels like something inside me is missing.

If I ever hurt you in any way, please believe me when I say I am truly sorry. I never wanted to be the reason for your pain. Sometimes people make mistakes not because they don't care, but because they don't know how to express their love properly.

From today, I don't want to just make empty promises.

I want to become someone you can truly believe in.

Someone who protects your heart instead of hurting it.

Someone who stays when things are difficult, not only when they are easy.

I will try to be patient with you.

I will respect your silence.

I will support you even when you push everyone away.

Because you matter to me more than you probably realize.

Sometimes I feel like God created my heart in a way that it only knows your name. How could I look at another when my soul already chose you? It feels impossible. Even the thought of loving someone else feels wrong to me.

You are the only person my heart truly wants.
